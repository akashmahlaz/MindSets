# Privacy Policy for MindHeal

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025

## Introduction

MindHeal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

**IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:** MindHeal is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of qualified health providers with questions regarding medical conditions. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, call your local emergency services immediately.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you register for MindHeal, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile photo (optional)
- Phone number (optional)
- Date of birth (for age verification)

### 2. Health Information (Optional)
With your explicit consent, we may collect:
- Mental health concerns or goals
- Counselor preferences
- Session notes (for counselors only)
- Treatment history

### 3. Communication Data
- Chat messages between users and counselors
- Video and audio call logs (metadata only, not recordings)
- Call duration and timestamps

### 4. Technical Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and diagnostic data

### 5. Location Data
We do NOT collect precise location data. General location (city/region) may be derived from IP address for counselor matching purposes only.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Connect you with licensed counselors
   - Facilitate video/audio calls and messaging
   - Process payments (when implemented)

2. **Improve Services**
   - Analyze app usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send appointment reminders
   - Notify about new messages or calls
   - Send important service updates

4. **Safety and Security**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal requirements

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

1. **Service Providers**
   - Firebase (Google) - Database and authentication
   - Stream.io - Video calling infrastructure
   - Cloud storage providers

2. **Counselors**
   - Your profile information with matched counselors
   - Communication history with your counselor

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - When required by law or court order
   - To protect safety and rights
   - In case of emergency or imminent harm

4. **With Your Consent**
   - Any other sharing will require explicit consent

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** All data transmitted is encrypted using TLS
- **Secure Storage:** Data at rest is encrypted
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments performed regularly

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active
- **Messages:** Retained for 90 days after account deletion
- **Call Logs:** Retained for 30 days
- **Health Information:** Deleted immediately upon account deletion

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your data
2. **Correction:** Update incorrect information
3. **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion
4. **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
5. **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@mindheal.app
